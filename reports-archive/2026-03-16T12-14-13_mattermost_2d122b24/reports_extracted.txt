2. **VULN-002 (MEDIUM-HIGH - CVSS 7.4)**: SSRF in OAuth Applications  
   - Location: `server/public/model/oauth.go` - `OAuthApp.IsValid()` method
   - Impact: OAuth callback URLs can point to internal services
   - Attack scenarios: Credential theft, internal network discovery, phishing
   - Related to VULN-001 (same root cause)
