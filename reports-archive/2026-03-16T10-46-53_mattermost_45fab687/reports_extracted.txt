### Findings Identified: 2 Potential Vulnerabilities

**1. VULN-001: SSRF in Outgoing Webhooks (HIGH Severity)**
- **Location:** `server/channels/app/webhook.go::doOutgoingWebhookRequest()`
- **Issue:** Outgoing webhooks make HTTP requests to user-supplied URLs without apparent SSRF protection
- **Impact:** Could allow access to cloud metadata services, internal network scanning, credential theft
- **CVSSv3 Estimate:** 8.6
- **Status:** Requires dynamic verification

**2. VULN-002: IDOR in Group Syncable Operations (MEDIUM-HIGH Severity)**
- **Location:** `server/channels/api4/group.go::linkGroupSyncable()`
- **Issue:** Group link operations may not validate permissions on target team/channel
- **Impact:** Could bypass team invitation system, grant unauthorized access to private channels
- **CVSSv3 Estimate:** 7.1
- **Status:** Requires dynamic verification
